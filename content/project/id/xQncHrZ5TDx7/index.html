1791


Description


Take command over a Regiment in the Custodia Republicana and use it in any way you see fit to utterly destroy the enemies that threaten your way of life.


Acquisition




Features


  • Top-down, 2D retro art style.
  • Roleplay as a Colonel in the Custodia Republicana and defeat all of Potentate's Enemies that threaten the great nation of Potensland!
  • Command your Companies across several Sectors through a Terminal interface brought to you by the Ministerium Scientiae et Technologiae.
  • Manage your HQ's and each Company's Supplies respectively.
  • Fight several Enemy factions on a number of different maps, terrains, and environments, each with its own set of rules!
  • Earn RRC and Research Units, as well as Level Up your résumé to unlock and equip your Companies with shiny new equipment.

Additional Notes


  • No internet connection is required.
  • The game is currently only supported on the Windows OS.
