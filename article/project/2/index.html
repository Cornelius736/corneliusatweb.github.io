What!?



1791 is a simple, top-down, 2D strategy game where you take the part of a Colonel in the Government's AF and are tasked with utterly annihilating the traitorous rebels.


Made in Godot, I aim to make the game be compatible with as many systems as possible. As far as operating systems go, currently, 1791 will only be on Windows, but that may change.
