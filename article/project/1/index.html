What!?



"Book" is my little, cutie patootie literary project, which I am yet to decide a name for...


It takes place during and right after the American Civil War, following the story of Henry Smith, a tired war volunteer and veteran who moved to the then Territory of Montana in search of solace after the war, only to find himself in a fierce feud between Cillian Finley and Aidan O'Connor.



Key Figures



Henry Smith


Hailing from Illinois, Henry served as a Union Army volunteer from 1862 to 1865. After the war, he found work as a ranch hand on a ranch owned by Cillian Finley in the newly founded Territory of Montana.

Cillian Finley


Born in Ireland, Cillian soon fled to the United States with his family. There, he made many a strong connection, notably John O'Mahony, which later allowed him to start his own ranching business.

vs.

Aidan O'Connor


Born in the United States after his parents immigrated there from Ireland. He was always considered a troublesome child, and he soon ended up in an orphanage. Years later, he would form a gang of thieves, murderers, rustlers, and rapists.



Pierre Bernard Beauregard


Was a private in the US Army until he joined the CSA, where he rose to become a General. Following the surrender at Appomattox, he, with a band of loyalists, fled to the Territory of Montana and started "rebuilding the Confederacy."
