A.P.M


Short for "Applicatio Productivitatis Multiusus," which roughly translates to "Multipurpose Productivity App." I created this app with the goal of combining some of the most useful general tools under a single roof.


I settled on a Checklist, Color Picker, Unit Converter, Ruler, Auto Clicker, Coin Flip, Lucky Wheel, Dice Roll, and a Tally.



Version: 1.0-B; Windows Only!
