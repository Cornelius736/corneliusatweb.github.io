About Me


Hi, I am a content creator, developer, and writer.


I make content on various social media platforms, primarily focusing on gaming and tech, as well as reviews, tutorials, essays, analysis videos, commentary, etc. related to each respective niche.


I started my developer career in Roblox Studio back in 2018, but soon started experimenting with Unreal Engine and Unity. It was about 2020 when I first learned about HTML and CSS, and I soon fell in love with it. Over the years, I've made many a website, and now I consider myself quite an expert in the field, actually! Recently, I decided to make a return to game development with my debut game, 1791, which is being made in Godot.


As of late 2025, I also decided to dip my fingers in writing and storytelling; thus, around December of 2025, I started work on my first novel, Ballads of Henry Smith, which will follow the main character, Henry Smith (who could've guessed?), as he spends four long years fighting in the grueling war known as the American Civil War, only to return to an empty home with no trace of his family. Will he manage to find them and when? Will it be too late? Well, I guess you'll need to read the book to find out! :D
